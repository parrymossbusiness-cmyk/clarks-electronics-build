Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact Clark's Electronic Solutions by phone, text, or our online booking form, we may collect your name, phone number, device information, and a description of the issue you're experiencing. This information is used solely to diagnose and repair your device and to communicate with you about your repair.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and perform repairs on your device
  • To contact you about the status of your repair
  • To provide quotes and follow up on service requests

Data Protection During Repairs

We take reasonable precautions to protect any personal data stored on devices brought in for repair. Your data stays safe throughout the repair process and is never accessed beyond what is necessary to complete the repair.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to complete a repair (such as ordering parts) or as required by law.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at 870-267-0678.
